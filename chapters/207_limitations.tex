\section{Limitations}

Look, I am super proud of this project, but let's be totally honest about its limitations. First off, the physics are heavily simplified. We don't have true soft-tissue deformation, meaning the colon walls don't squish and stretch when the endoscope pushes against them. Secondly, our polyps are basically just geometric sphere-proxies under the hood, not fully anatomical soft-body simulations. Finally, training a policy purely in a simulator is one thing, but deploying it in a real clinical setting (what the AI folks call sim-to-real transfer) is a massive hurdle that often breaks brittle Deep RL policies.
