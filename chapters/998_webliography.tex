This section lists the online resources, datasets, and digital textbooks referenced throughout the project.

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Deep Learning} by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville. \\ Available at: \url{https://www.deeplearningbook.org/}
    
    \item \textbf{Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction} (2nd Edition) by Richard S. Sutton and Andrew G. Barto. \\ Available at: \url{http://incompleteideas.net/book/the-book-2nd.html}
    
    \item \textbf{Colorectal Cancer Fact Sheet (GLOBOCAN 2022)} by the International Agency for Research on Cancer (World Health Organization). \\ Available at: \url{https://gco.iarc.fr/today/fact-sheets-cancers}
    
    \item \textbf{StatPearls: Colorectal Polyps} by Amy Leslie et al. \\ Available at: \url{https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK430761/}
    
    \item \textbf{Diagnostic Accuracy of AI for Colorectal Polyps} published in the Journal of Medical Internet Research. \\ Available at: \url{https://www.jmir.org/2021/7/e27370/}
\end{itemize}
